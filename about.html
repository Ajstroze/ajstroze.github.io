Background

I graduated from Earlham College in 2022 with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. During my time there I worked for the CS department as a system administrator, where I gained my first direct experience with formal security and administration practices.

That experience led me to pursue a Master's in Information Security from Carnegie Mellon, where I met a lot of great people, both peers and professors who had already made significant impact in the field. During my master's program I interned with LexisNexis on their Security Operations team.

Upon graduating in 2024 I joined LexisNexis full time where I have the opportunity to lead projects that have a direct impact on the security posture of the organization. I am an energetic, curious and driven learner who is passionate about learning the ins and outs of technology and security. I thrive in environments that challenge me and love collaborating with others.
